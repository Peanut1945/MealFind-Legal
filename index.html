Made by students, for healthy living

We're a team of passionate students who believe everyone deserves access to nutritious meals that actually fit real life.

MealFind was created by two university students with a shared passion for food and a simple goal: to make healthy eating more accessible, affordable and enjoyable.

The idea was born in university halls, where we saw first-hand how many students struggled with two common challenges: finding meals that fit their budget, and knowing what to cook. Too often, healthy eating is seen as expensive, complicated or time-consuming. We wanted to change that.

"Healthy body, healthy mind." It's a simple phrase, but one that has stayed with us — good food is about much more than calories or macros. It's about feeling your best and building habits that help you live a healthier, happier life. — A lesson from Nani 💚

As the cost of living continues to rise, this challenge extends far beyond students. Whether your goal is to save money, eat more protein or fibre, meal-prep more effectively, or simply discover new meals you'll enjoy, MealFind is designed to help.
